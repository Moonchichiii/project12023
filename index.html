Reasons why ?

I created a website to share my passion for the Day of the Dead festival - a unique and fascinating celebration that I vividly remember from my travels to Mexico over a decade ago.

Originally, I planned to create a website about Alexander the Great's mother, but couldn't find any pictures online, which stressed me out.

Reflecting on what inspired me, I decided to explore the history, traditions, and beauty of the Day of the Dead festival instead.

Small introduction documentary

This documentary explores the history and evolution of the Mexican Day of the Dead - from its pagan roots to the multifaceted observances of today.

Artists, curators, and paleobiologists share their perspectives on how the living remember and honor the dead through cooking, eating, drinking, music-making, and decorating graves.

The documentary emphasizes the significance of the love and spirit that is shared in these rituals.
